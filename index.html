About Me

I am a Computer Science student specializing in Cyber Security at the British University of Bahrain. I have a passion for ethical hacking, digital forensics, and developing secure mobile applications. One of my major projects includes developing BUB Threads, a student-centered social media platform designed for academic engagement and student interaction. This project allowed me to apply skills in Swift, Firebase, and UI/UX design while addressing real-world challenges in security and user experience. I am continuously expanding my knowledge in penetration testing, malware analysis, and secure application development, with hands-on experience using tools like Wireshark, Metasploit, Volatility, and Oracle VirtualBox.
